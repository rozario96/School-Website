MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

Dear Parents & Students,

As the Director for the LinkTech School I am pleased to write this welcome message to you and our wider community.

With its delightful, hardworking and friendly student body, supportive and involved parent community as well as a dedicated, loyal and prefessional faculty and staff we have a wonderful community of which we can all be really proud.

We have worked hard to develop a set of guiding statements for the school. These include a set of values which we, as a community, aspire to live by and a set of measurable objectives which gives us a framework for all that we do at the school. These values and objectives are derived from our mission and are published on our website.

Our website provides a window into the wonderful school that is LinkTech School. I would encourage anyone interested in becoming part of our community to contact our Reception to make an appointment to visit.

Best Regards,

Kafi Mannan
